# PetID

QR-linked pet ID tag pages with clear, editable information to help pets get home safely.

---

## 🐾 How It Works

Each pet has its own webpage that can be accessed by scanning a QR code on their tag.

The page provides:
- Immediate contact information  
- Pet photos, vehicle details, and travel information  
- Clear instructions if the pet is found  
- Real-time status updates  
- Location details if the pet is lost  

This allows anyone who finds your pet to quickly understand what to do and how to help.

---

## 📱 What Makes This Different

- No apps required  
- No logins required  
- Fully editable at any time  
- Works instantly when scanned  
- Designed for real-world situations  

---

## 🛠️ Built For

- Pet owners who need more than just a name and phone number on a tag  
- Pet owners who want a system they fully control  
- Owners who do not want to share personal information with third-party services  
- Situations where tags alone are not enough  
- Especially ideal for full-time RV travel, camping, and changing locations  
- Real-time updates without reprinting tags  

---

## ⚠️ Important

These pages are designed to be:
- Fast to read  
- Easy to understand  
- Immediately actionable  

All critical information is placed at the top for quick access.

---

## 🐶 Structure

Each pet has its own page organized under your main directory.

Each page includes:
- Pet photos  
- Contact information  
- Behavior and handling notes  
- Medical and vet information  
- Lost mode with last known location  

---

## ✏️ Editing

All pages are fully editable.

You can update:
- Pet’s name  
- Contact information  
- Current status  
- Location  
- Travel details  
- Emergency notes  
- And more  

Changes go live immediately after saving.

---

## ❤️ Purpose

This system exists for one reason:

Most pet ID services charge ongoing monthly or yearly fees just to keep your pet’s information active.

You should not need to give away personal information or rely on third-party services just to keep your pet safe.

This approach keeps control in your hands while providing detailed, up-to-date information when it matters most.
